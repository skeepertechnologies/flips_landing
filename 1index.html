F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions about FLIPS

FLIPS stands for Flood Level Information and Prediction System. It utilizes advanced data analytics and hydrological models to predict water levels in rivers and streams, providing timely warnings to potentially affected areas.

FLIPS predictions are based on a combination of historical data, real-time weather observations, and hydrological modeling techniques. While highly accurate, users should always prepare for possible variations due to unforeseen weather changes.

Yes, FLIPS provides an API that allows you to integrate its data with other software applications for enhanced situational awareness and response planning in emergency management or urban planning.

FLIPS provides various alerts including immediate flood warnings, water level changes, and long-term flood risk assessments based on weather forecasts and geographical data.

You can subscribe to FLIPS alerts through our website. Simply create an account and configure your alert preferences based on your location and the specific types of information you are interested in.
